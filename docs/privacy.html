Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 June 2026

Tradie Invoice helps businesses create invoices and quotes from messages, voice notes and uploaded business documents.

Information we process

We may process chat identifiers, business details, customer details supplied by users, invoice and quote content, uploaded files, logos and service usage records.

How information is used

Information is used to create documents, identify the correct business profile, provide support, enforce service limits, maintain security and improve reliability.

Uploaded documents

Users should upload only documents they are authorised to use. Extracted details must be reviewed before they are saved.

Service providers

We may use hosting, messaging, document-processing and artificial-intelligence service providers where required to operate the service.

Retention and deletion

Information may be retained while an account is active and for a reasonable period required for support, security and legal obligations. Users may request correction or deletion.

Security

Reasonable safeguards are used, but no internet service can guarantee absolute security.

Contact

Privacy enquiries can be submitted through the official Tradie Invoice communication channel.
